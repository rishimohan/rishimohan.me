The app allows users to:

- **Upload or Capture Photos**: Drag & drop or take a photo directly.
- **Customize**: Add a caption and pick a background color.
- **Generate**: Transform the photo into a polaroid-style image using Orshot.
- **Download**: Save individual polaroids or download all as a ZIP.

[View the full source code on GitHub](https://github.com/rishimohan/instant-polaroid)
