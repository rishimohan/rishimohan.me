In a world leaning on wanting things fast, this track, both the lyrics and the picturization puts a paddle on right things

To wait, to see, to steal glances, to be close, to just be around, to talk through eyes, to appreciate the being, to just live specially little moments of life with someone you like, is an art we appreciate less

And I mean not in a way where you delibirately do things, I mean in the most natural of being, things you don't do, things that happen, as natural as you are, and yet their bliss transcends in you for eternity

I'll cherish it by putting it in my little corner, and will come back to this black and white picturization, correctly so, as a memory of way of living we might lose
